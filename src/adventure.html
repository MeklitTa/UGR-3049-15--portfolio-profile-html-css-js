Meskel Festival

Meskel is one of the most remarkable festivals in Ethiopia, celebrated with great reverence by followers of the Ethiopian Orthodox faith. It commemorates the discovery of the True Cross and is marked by vibrant religious ceremonies, the lighting of the Demera bonfire, and cultural festivities that symbolize unity and spiritual devotion.
